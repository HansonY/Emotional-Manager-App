This app is developed and operated by an individual developer.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information necessary to provide core app functionality. This may include:
- A unique user identifier provided by Sign in with Apple
- Email address (including Apple private relay email, if enabled)
- Subscription status and basic usage data

## 2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to:
- Authenticate users
- Maintain user accounts
- Sync subscription status
- Provide app features and services

## 3. Data Storage

User data is securely stored using Supabase cloud services. We take reasonable measures to protect user information.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the app or comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Account and Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and associated data directly within the app.
